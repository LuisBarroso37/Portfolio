About Me

My name is Luís Barroso and I am 24 years old. I'm from Porto, Portugal and I am a civil engineer. Currently I am working as a scaffolding calculator for KAEFER in Belgium and live in Ghent.

When I got into university, I didn't manage to get in the course I wanted at the time (Electrotechnical Engineering) and got in my second choice (Civil Engineering), which I ended up liking and finding it quite interesting. Despite this and currently working in this field, I feel that this is not what I really want to do in my life.

So, a couple of months ago, out of nowhere, I found CODING.

And I´m very glad I did! Ever since I made the decision to start learning how to code, I feel like I found what I want to do in my life. I am enjoying working on small projects a lot, trying to find solutions for the problems that I encounter using my knowledge and searching for information online which allows me to learn a lot in the process.

So far, I've learned HTML, CSS and Full Stack Javascript. In the Front End I can use vanilla Javascript and React to make interactive webpages. In the Back End I know how to work with API's using Node.js, Express, MongoDB and Mongoose, although still at a basic level.
